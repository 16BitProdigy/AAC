What We Do

Commercial and International joint Ventures Services The Firm provides a broad range of Commercial and Corporate Services to multi-national companies in the Oil and Gas, Telecommunications, Banking and Commodities Sector of the Nigerian Market.
Corporate finance and Banking services Our Firm has gained considerable experience in advising on corporate finance and banking services with lawyers who are well versed in commercial law. we specialize in commercial agreements that provide the structure under which business activities are conducted locally.
Litigation The Firm works closely with retained consultants to provide our clients with litigation counseling. We have represented clients in a wide range of civil matters including: Banking, Telecommunications, International Trade, Intellectual Property and Land matters.
Property Consultancy and Management The Firm acts principally in this area for Corporate Clients, Multilateral Institutions, Embassies, Banks, Private Individuals and Estates. We advise routinely on a variety of commercial and residential lettings and investments. The Firm also has extensive experience in the negotiation of asset acquisition, management and disposition.
Private Client Services We provide a wide range of personalized services to our clients who need help in niche areas which are not covered in our other areas of specialization.
International Representation We help our clients with processing expatriate quota positions, obtaining work permit for expatriates working in Nigeria, procurement of Combined Expatriate Residence Permit and Alien Card (CERPAC), importation of investment capital and repatriation of profits for foreign investors.
Business Services The Firm offers the following related services to businesses currently operating or intending to operate in Nigeria: Enquiries/Investigation, Meetings, Investment/Joint Venture Advice, Immigration and Political Liason.

Meet Our Partners

Olusegun Ashaye is an experienced Barrister and has represented a wide range of clients ranging from multi-national corporations, banks, financial institutions and overseas law firms. He advises on all aspects of Nigerian litigation practice and procedure. His areas of specialisation include commercial practice, Banking, corporate recovery and insolvency, company and partnership disputes. He graduated from Ogun State University in 1996 and was called to the Nigerian Bar in 1998. He possesses extensive experience in corporate and commercial practice and is involved in negotiation of joint ventures. He is also well versed in real property practice and advises on all aspects of property law with particular emphasis on acquisitions, conveyancing, leases and mortgages.

Olusegun Ashaye

Barrister Gbenga Durojaiye is a partner in the firm’s litigation and dispute resolution practice. He obtained his LL.B (Hons) degree from Olabisi Onabanjo University Ogun State in 2008 and was called to the Nigerian Bar in 2010. He has acquired a wealth of experience from various fields of law such as labour law, family and real estate laws, contract and commercial law etc. He has developed considerable skills in legal drafting and advocacy. He is particularly well versed in the area of family and probate practice. Gbenga heads the dispute resolution team of the firm with a unique style in mediation which has resulted into out-of-court settlements even in highly sensitive and difficult cases.

About Us

  AIDEN ALLAN & CO was established when its principal partner pulled out from an active and brilliant law firm with a significant record of successes for more than a decade (ADENIYI, ASHAYE & CO).

  Upon a peaceful determination of the partnership agreement between the two managing partners of Adeniyi, Ashaye & Co, AIDEN ALLAN & CO was established in association with seasoned legal practitioners and exceptional administrative personnel. The Firm’s strong reputation stems largely from its recognized ability to identify and respond readily to significant changes in local and international Commercial Markets and the tested experience, competence and cohesion of its legal team.

  The Firm has correspondents in Europe, Africa and continues to expand to meet the growth in volume, size, and complexity of transactions amidst an increasingly intricate legislative and regulatory environment in Nigeria. Our team of dedicated legal practitioners have a unique understanding of all the complexities of the law, using our extensive familiarity with the relevant authorities and processes; we help deliver speedy resolution/solution for a multitude of legal procedures faced by our clients.

  We are not only committed to building a world class Law Firm with super legal practitioners but also to providing quality and comprehensive legal services to all our clients without discrimination as we settle for nothing less than excellence in our service delivery.
